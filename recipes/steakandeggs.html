Steak and Eggs

Juicy tasty steak with runny eggs to soak them

Another easy and quick meal. One that will have your taste buds firing on all cylinders

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Eggs
  • Frozen Veggies
  • Mushrooms
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook your steak on medium heat
  2. Add mushrooms to the steak and frying pan
  3. Chuck your frozen veggies in a container and microwave them
  4. As your veggies and steak heat up start using another pan to cook your eggs
  5. Once complete put your steaks on to a plate and over them with your eggs! The more the better.
  6. Add the mushrooms and some cherry tomatoes on top!
  7. Pour an even amount of protein shake into each container. You may need to use a spoon to mix the oats with the milk.
